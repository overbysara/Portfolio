About Portfolio

I work in philanthropy as a Program Officer at the Colorado Health Foundation. The work gives me the opportunity to meet organizational and community leaders across Southwestern Colorado, including the beautiful towns of Cortez, Durango, and Salida.

At the Foundation, I have provided $1 million to telehealth projects to increase access to health care in rural areas, several million dollars for renovations of buildings and playgrounds, and support for community groups working on advocacy and social justice issues.

I am passionate about the work of the public and nonprofit sectors, and the potential for digital tools and data to contribute to the public good.
